Pre Weddings

Pre-wedding shoot sounds fancy and a lot of couples think it is unnecessary and expensive too. But the truth is, a pre-wedding photo shoot has a number of perks that will help you. Here are some of the key benefits of choosing a pre-wedding photography. It helps build a rapport between you and your photographer






Haldi ceremony

Haldi ceremonies are no more only about the ritualistic application of haldi to the bride and groom but, it has amplified into being a fun-filled party in itself. From water showers to color and flower holi being incorporated, haldi has become one super essential and unskippable function that couples and their fams just can't do without.

Mehandi

Mehendi ceremony is unquestionably the most fun, colorful and vibrant of all the wedding ceremonies and there are no two thoughts about that. Right from a happy themed decor, fun games and an unusual food & dessert spread to quirky and aesthetic favors and peppy mehndi looks of brides, grooms and the guests, the effervescent vibes of a mehndi ceremony are all captivating and heart-tugging.






Sangeet ceremony

It is no news that Sangeet ceremonies at Indian weddings have now become a lot like full-fledged productions, with a scale as massive as that of a Bollywood award show nights. Larger-than-life stages are set up, choreographers are hired, back-to-back performances set the floor ablaze and a team of videographers is on action to film it in a way that it looks straight out from a Yash Raj banner movie!

Weddings

Your wedding photographs are the most precious keepsakes from your wedding that you cherish eternally. And while every photographer can click great pictures for your wedding, there are only a few who can actually turn those soulful moments into an everlasting memory. Which is why for a day as special as your wedding, an occasion that only comes once in a lifetime, you ought to have a photographer that has an eye for capturing even the most trivial moments like a dream.






Vidai ceremony

Vidaai is undoubtedly one of the most soul-stirring and extensively photographed moments of a wedding. It's the last ritual of the wedding where the bride bids a teary farewell to her family and embarks on a new journey with her husband. Even though it is majorly denoted with quivering smiles and teary eyes, a lot of brides nowadays are happily enjoying every bit of their vidaai ceremony and incorporating fun & interesting elements to quirk it up. While some are leaving off in fascinating vintage cars & royal carriages, others are infusing the charm of sparklers and flower showers to their vidaai.
